About Us


We are: Akshat Gupta, Rishita Borah, Mischa Thomas,
Akriti Kataria, and Tanish Mathur


We all are 8th grade students in Oakridge International
School Bengaluru, and we strive to improve your
driving experience on the roads of Bangalore.
We envision a city where traffic is an issue of the past.

This website is designed to make traffic caused by construction
a thing of the past. We plan on achieving this by letting
users inform others in the community about certain construction areas.

Happy Travelling!
